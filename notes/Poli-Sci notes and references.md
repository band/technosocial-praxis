# Poli-Sci notes and references

This page is an umbrella for notes about political science and political practices.  

- **Anocracy**, or **semi-democracy**, is a form of government that is loosely defined as part democracy and part dictatorship, or as a "regime that mixes democratic with autocratic features". (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anocracy) (2025-04-27)
